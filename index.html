Now What Is The Mindset


The mindset is a belief system that governs the way a person act.Esssentially,a belief system is made up from the kinds of places,peoples and information that one is exposed to.The places and people you spend most of the time and around with,are the ones that will mostly determines the information that you take up to your believe system.

Types Of Mindset 1:Fixed mindset:People with fixed mindset believes that thier talents,abilities,intelligence are from fixed trait.They also believe that talent alone is enough to gain success.

2:Growth mindset:People with growth mindset believe so much in them selves.They are people who believe that their ability can be develop through dedication and hardwork.They often view challenges as opportunities to unleash their hidden potential.

Hope by now you can discern which category you belong to.
